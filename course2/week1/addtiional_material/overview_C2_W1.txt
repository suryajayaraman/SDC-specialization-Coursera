Least Squares

y = x + v

y = measurements
v = measurement noise
x = ground truth value

y1 = x +v1
y2 = x +v2
y3 = x +v3

e1^2 = (y1 - x)^2


https://phet.colorado.edu/sims/html/least-squares-regression/latest/least-squares-regression_en.html


https://textbooks.math.gatech.edu/ila/least-squares.html

e2^2 = (y2 - x)^2 and so on.

Objective of LS is to find x such that it minimises (e1^2 + e2^2 + e3^2)
